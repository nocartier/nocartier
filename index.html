I am a doctor in mathematical computer science at the Université Paris-Saclay, in team GALaC of the LISN. My PhD directors were Florent Hivert and Vincent Pilaud.

I work in algebraic combinatorics, and I am currently studying the lattice properties of subword complexes in Coxeter groups.

Research

My PhD

Projet SuCRe: Subword Complexes Realizations, directed by Florent Hivert and Vincent Pilaud.

Subword complexes on Coxeter groups are objects that were introduced by Knutson and Miller to study Schubert polynomials. They are a natural generalization of the study of sorting networks. By choosing a certain family of sorting networks and studying the ways of sorting a specific permutation on them, we find a new realization of some already well-known lattices, including the Tamari lattice. Those lattices have in commong that they are quotient of the weak order on permutation, and we are now studying the necessary conditions to find other quotients of the weak order on Coxeter group in various subword complexes.
